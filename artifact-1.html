Artifact 1

Description

This artifact come from a class I taught (ETR 440) for the Education Technology, Research and Assessment department of Northern Illinois University. This specific item is the final project instructions and rubric for the students of these classes. I created this document to guide so that I could evaluate their learning throughout the semester and give them credit for their hard work. I included this specifically because it provides an example of my ability to create evaluations for students and ethically evaluate their abilities in a course.

Objective 2 - Design and/or select appropriate assessment or evaluation tools for a given educational problem

This item is an evaluation tool that I designed for a course. It provides several key elements of evaluation. The document provides students with clear instructions on what is expected of them. It gives the students exact places to retrieve any information they need to complete this task. It reminds students of certain key topics I will be evaluating for. Finally, it provides them with a rubric on which this assignment will be evaluated using.

Objective 3 - Distinguish between ethical and unethical behavior when conducting educational research or evaluations

This item is important to ethical and unethical evaluation because it requires that the grader be sure to not incorporate any bias or preconceived notions about the student they have when grading it. This is why, when grading assignments I ensure that I never look at the names of the students I am grading. It is important that all students get an equal chance to be evaluated for their work based solely off its merits and nothing else.
